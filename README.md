##  SQL EDA Project – Global Layoffs Analysis

##  Overview
This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a global layoffs dataset using SQL. The goal is to uncover patterns and insights related to company layoffs across industries, countries, and time periods.

---

## Tools Used
- SQL (MySQL)
- Dataset: world_layoffs

---

## Project Highlights

###  Data Exploration
- Explored the complete dataset to understand structure and key attributes  
- Identified maximum layoffs and maximum layoff percentage  

---

###  Company Analysis
- Identified companies that laid off **100% of their workforce (complete shutdowns)**  
- Found companies with the highest total layoffs  
- Calculated average layoff percentage per company  
- Ranked companies based on total layoffs  

---

###  Industry Analysis
- Analyzed total layoffs across different industries  
- Identified industries most affected by layoffs  

---

###  Time-Based Analysis
- Studied layoffs trends over different years  
- Identified yearly peaks in layoffs  
- Analyzed monthly layoffs patterns to understand fluctuations over time  

---

###  Trend Analysis
- Calculated cumulative layoffs over time to understand overall progression  

---

### Advanced Analysis
- Ranked companies within each year based on layoffs  
- Identified top companies with highest layoffs per year  

---

##  Key Insights
- Some companies experienced complete workforce shutdowns (100% layoffs)  
- Certain industries were more affected than others  
- Layoffs showed clear spikes during specific years  
- A small number of companies contributed to most layoffs  
- Overall layoffs showed an increasing trend over time  

---
## Skills Demonstrated
-Data Exploration using SQL
-Aggregation and grouping techniques
-Time-series analysis
-Trend identification
-Window functions for ranking and rolling calculations
-Business insight extraction from raw data

##  Conclusion
This project demonstrates how SQL can be used to perform real-world data analysis and extract meaningful business insights from raw datasets. It highlights trends in global layoffs across companies, industries, and time periods.
